ABOUT US

Our Company

Bluejays Travel and Tours is newly established local tour providers in Cavinti, Laguna. It was founded on August 1, 2017 as start-up travel and tour agency with add-on travel-related assistance services. And one of affiliates member of Sarap Mag Biyahe (SMB) Travel and Tours.

Bluejays is a group of friends (barkadahan) name in town of Cavinti were the founder is member. He just borrowed the name and used as his business name. The founder loves to travel and explore unfamiliar territories.

Appreciated natural wonders of nature as well as the unique delicacies and cultures of every place he visited.

These are the reasons why Bluejays Travel and Tours came into Tourism Market to share the uniqueness, amazing features and explore more attractions outside and inside of the Philippines.
